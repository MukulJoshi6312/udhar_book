Hello,
This application will change your offline ledger khata and 
convert it into a digital ledger with many features.
Replace your traditional credit ledger account with a new digital 
ledger cash book.
It is 100% free, secure and secure for all types of businesses to 
maintain their customer accounts.
Shop owners can also use this app to record credit and debit 
transactions for their trusted customers
